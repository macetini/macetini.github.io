Bender Shader

A custom vertex shader created in Unity to simulate bending effects and animations on 3D meshes. This project was inspired by Curves and Splines tutorial by Jasper Flick of Catlike Coding.

While the project has mostly been archived due to Unity's subsequent introduction of its own built-in Spline System, it serves as a strong demonstration of shader programming and mesh deformation and animation techniques.
