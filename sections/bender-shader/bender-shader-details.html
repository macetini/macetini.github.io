Overview

In early 2010s I created a custom shader in Unity to simulate bending meshes along the spline. This shader deforms the vertices of 3D models based on their position relative to a defined spline curve, allowing for dynamic and interactive mesh deformations.

The spline curve is defined using a series of points and populated with a prefab.

Both Spline and Prefab are defined in the Unity Inspector UVCurve.cs component.

User can populate the spline with prefabs and configure them as needed.

Static Curve

The result is a static model composed of a series of meshes along the curve.
