Overview

Back in 2020, I participated in a 24-hour game jam and was a part of team that developed an Unity 2D platformer. The game challenges players to navigate through an infinite random generated level filled with obstacles and enemies. Despite the tight timeframe, we managed to implement core gameplay mechanics, design level, and create art assets. The experience honed my skills in rapid prototyping, time management, and creative problem-solving under pressure.

The game was built using Unity, leveraging its 2D capabilities to create engaging platforming mechanics. We focused on simple controls and responsive gameplay to ensure a fun experience within the limited time. The Player horizontal movement was controlled with a burst of force by Left and Right Mouse Clicks, while jumping was handled with the Spacebar. The level generation algorithm created a series of platforms, obstacles, and enemies, ensuring that each play through offered a unique challenge.
